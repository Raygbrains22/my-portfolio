Money Tracker
Expense Tracker is a user-friendly web application built with React.js, designed to streamline financial management. Its intuitive interface allows users to effortlessly add transactions, monitor balances, and analyze income versus expenses. With the ability to view balances in multiple currencies and delete transactions with ease, Expense Tracker offers a convenient solution for effective budget tracking.
Project Details / Background
Expense Tracker: Empowering Financial Management Expense Tracker is a sophisticated web-based application meticulously crafted with React.js to streamline financial management processes. Seamlessly record transactions, gain insights into your financial status, and maintain an organized record of your expenses effortlessly. With its intuitive interface and robust feature set, Expense Tracker offers a convenient solution for individuals looking to take control of their finances effectively.
Key Features: Transaction Management: Effortlessly add, view, and delete transactions with ease, ensuring accurate record-keeping. Real-time Balance Updates: Stay informed about your financial status with real-time balance updates, providing a comprehensive breakdown of income and expenses. Multi-currency Support: Convert the balance to different currencies for international users, ensuring universal accessibility and usability.
Technology Stack: Frontend: React.js, Chart.js State Management: Context API & React Hooks Styling: CSS & Bootstrap Deployments: Expense Tracker is live and accessible at https://money-tracker-group.netlify.app/. Contributing: Contributions to Expense Tracker are welcomed! If you find any issues or have suggestions for improvements, feel free to open an issue or submit a pull request.
