Tomato Flix
Discover movies, explore industry news, and revisit past searches with ease. Powered by Bootstrap, JQuery, and integrated with GNews and Rapid API.
Project Details / Background
Movie Search and News Aggregator Web Application Welcome to our dynamic Movie Search and News Aggregator web application! This versatile tool is designed to provide users with a seamless experience for exploring movies, accessing industry news, and revisiting past searches. Developed collaboratively by a team of skilled developers, our application harnesses the power of modern web technologies to deliver a robust and user-friendly platform.
Key Features: Movie Search: Users can easily search for their favorite movies by entering the title into the search field and hitting the "Squish Time" tomato button. Utilizing the Rapid API, our application retrieves comprehensive data about the chosen selection, including ratings, reviews, cast information, and more. Sound Integration: Enhancing the user experience, our application features sound integration, allowing users to play or pause related soundtracks directly within the interface. This feature adds an immersive element to the movie exploration process. Industry News Aggregator: Stay informed with the latest industry news and updates by clicking the "Display Relevant News Articles" button. Our application fetches headlines from reputable sources across the web, providing users with timely insights into the world of cinema. Clicking on these headlines redirects users to the respective article URLs for further reading. Search History: Never lose track of your previous searches with our convenient search history feature. The "History" column displays the five latest searches, enabling users to revisit and explore past selections with ease. Credits: This project was made possible through the collaborative efforts of our talented team of developers: DrWho1369 harrynewt0n HIYASHE06 Nukte-y Raygbrains22
Technologies Used: Bootstrap 5.3: for creating responsive and visually appealing UI elements. JQuery 3.5: for simplifying DOM manipulation and event handling. GNews API: for fetching industry-relevant news articles from across the web. Rapid API: for retrieving comprehensive movie data and information.
